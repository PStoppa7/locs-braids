Booking Policy

  • A deposit is required to secure your booking.
  • Please arrive on time. A 15-minute grace period is allowed.
  • No extra guests during appointments.
  • Rescheduling should be done at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Please notify us immediately in case of cancellations.

Appointment Policy

  • Changes & Cancellations: If any changes need to be made to your appointment, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance in order to avoid forfeiting your deposit.
  • Deposit: To secure your spot, a non-refundable 50% deposit will be collected at the time of booking. Once payment is received into our account, your appointment will be confirmed.

Reminders

  • Your appointment will be cancelled if you arrive 15 minutes late. Please keep in mind that the deposit is non-refundable.
  • For purposes of safety and sanitation, we cannot allow more than one guest per room.
  • Please arrive without any eye makeup and do not curl your lashes on the day of your appointment.
